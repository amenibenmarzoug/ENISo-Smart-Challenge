Road To Utopia

Tout Terrain

The course is composed of many different missions. The remote-controlled robots have to go through it and get as far as possible from the starting line. First, the robots will have to face the disaster era where the damage is always chasing them. The only way to survive is to find the magical "Time machine" which will pave for you the way to reach "Utopia" , the stunning city where you can reboot your future and live peacefully ever after. At the beginning of each round, Four robots will be placed at the starting line and will go through this epic adventure. The qualification depends on the Approval Score , the duration as well as the marks scored during the race.

Bring Back The Big Bang

Bateau Junior

The Big Bang is an event resembling a huge explosion that happened about 15 billion years ago. According to the theory of the Big Bang, this explosion was responsible for the expansion of the Universe and its current structure. The Big Bang, for example, gave birth to the galaxies, formed the planets, the stars... a lot of scientists think that there was nothing before the Big Bang. Your mission is to reach the core of the universe through the maze to bring back the Big Bang so it’s up to you to create the world you imagine.
