About the ESC

ENISo Smart Challenge, created in 2014, is a robotics competition organised by ENISo TEAM. Since the previous edition, the organization of this event is carried out in partnership with the "Advanced Mechanics Department".

This is a fun, scientific and technical challenge, the largest of its kind in the Sahel region, where you can meet the robotics enthusiasts. Our event counts currently five areas of competition :

  • Armageddon (Death-Ring)
  • The Martian Shelter (Line Follower)
  • Road To Utopia (All Terrain)
  • Lost City Raiders (Senior Boat)
  • Bring Back The Big Bang (Junior Boat)
